# Introduction

## What is Creopse?

**Creopse** is a content management system that allows you to manage all the content of your website from a simple and intuitive administration interface.

## What you can do

Once logged in, Creopse gives you access to everything you need to keep your site up to date on a daily basis:

- **[Overview](sections/dashboard)** — The dashboard gives you a snapshot of your site's activity as soon as you log in.
- **[Media Library](sections/media-library)** — Centralize all your images and videos in one place and reuse them on any page without uploading the same file twice.
- **[Content Management](sections/content-management)** — Create, edit, and organize your site's pages. Each page can be published, unpublished, or reordered as needed.
- **[Visual Editor](sections/visual-editor)** — Edit your page content section by section directly from the interface. Your changes only affect the content, never the design.
- **[News Management](sections/news-management)** — Create and publish news articles with their title, content, cover image, and publication date.
- **[User Management](sections/user-management)** — Add and manage the people who have access to the administration.
- **[Access Management](sections/access-management)** — Define who can do what within the interface.
- **[Account Information](sections/account-info)** — View and update your personal information.
- **[Extensions](sections/plugins)** — Discover additional features available on your site.
- **[Settings](sections/settings)** — Configure your site's general options: language, navigation, and other basic settings.

## When in doubt

If something does not display as expected after a change, contact your administrator before making any further modifications.
